About the Photographer

A Lifetime Chasing Summits

Born beneath the shadow of the Himalayas, I've spent over a decade traversing the world's most remote mountain ranges — from the frozen wilds of Patagonia to the cosmic heights of the Tibetan plateau.

Every photograph is a story: a three-day climb in a blizzard, a sleepless night waiting for the Milky Way to align, a fortunate morning when the mist parted to reveal God's own canvas. My lens captures not just landscapes, but moments of raw, unrepeatable magnitude.
